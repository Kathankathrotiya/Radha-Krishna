My Dearest Radha,

Can you believe it — eleven whole years? Eleven years since the universe, in all its infinite wisdom, decided to write our names together in the stars. I remember the very first moment with a clarity no amount of time can blur — the way you smiled, the way the air around me suddenly felt different, as if the world had exhaled and quietly made room for something sacred to begin.

These years have been our greatest adventure. We have laughed until our sides ached and held each other through nights that felt impossible to survive. Through every season — the golden warmth of our best days, the fierce storms we weathered side by side — I have watched you grow into someone more extraordinary with every passing year. And each time, without fail, I fall a little more in love.

Like Radha and Krishna across the endless gardens of Vrindavan, our love has echoed through every chapter of this life. I find you in every song that carries me home, in every sunset that reminds me of what truly matters, in every ordinary morning made luminous simply because you are near.

The promises we have made to each other — spoken on careful evenings, whispered in the dark, sealed in moments too tender for words — I hold every single one in my heart as the most precious things I will ever own. They are the roots that have kept us standing through everything.

And so I offer every prayer I have to the One who blessed Radha and Krishna with a bond beyond all time — asking Him, with everything in me, to keep us woven together. Not just in this life, but in every life, every universe, across every turn of the eternal wheel.

You are my home. You are my temple. You are my forever.

Here is to eleven beautiful, irreplaceable years — and to the infinite ones already waiting ahead for us.

With every heartbeat and every breath,
~ Your Kanha
